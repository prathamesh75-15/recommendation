Eligibility Criteria

  • Farmers must own or lease agricultural land.
  • Applicable to small, medium, and large-scale farms.
  • Insurance coverage based on land size and crop type.

How to File a Claim

  1. Report the incident within 48 hours of damage.
  2. Submit required documents (land ownership, crop loss proof, etc.).
  3. Inspection by authorized personnel.
  4. Claim approval and compensation processing.

How to Choose the Right Plan

  1. Assess your farm’s risk factors (climate, pests, theft, etc.).
  2. Compare coverage options and premiums.
  3. Check for government subsidies and benefits.
  4. Apply online or contact an insurance provider.
