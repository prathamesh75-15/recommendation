Importance of Weather Forecasting

Weather forecasting is crucial for farmers as it helps them make informed decisions about planting, irrigation, and harvesting. Accurate forecasts can lead to better crop yields and reduced losses.

Types of Weather Data

  • Temperature: Affects plant growth and development.
  • Humidity: Influences transpiration and disease risk.
  • Precipitation: Essential for irrigation planning.
  • Wind Speed: Affects pollination and crop protection.
  • Frost Alerts: Important for protecting sensitive crops.

Farming Tips Based on Weather

  • High Rainfall: Consider planting water-tolerant crops like rice.
  • Dry Weather: Use drip irrigation to conserve water.
  • Cold Conditions: Protect crops using greenhouse or mulch covers.
  • Stormy Weather: Secure loose farm equipment to prevent damage.
