Fasal Bima Yojana

Agriculture insurance scheme to protect crops against natural calamities.

Eligibility: All farmers growing notified crops.
Benefits: Coverage for crop loss due to adverse weather conditions.

Soil Health Card Scheme

Provides farmers with information on soil health and nutrient management.

Eligibility: All farmers.
Benefits: Helps in improving soil fertility and crop productivity.

Pradhan Mantri Krishi Sinchai Yojana

Aims to improve irrigation facilities and water conservation.

Eligibility: All farmers.
Benefits: Enhances irrigation efficiency and crop yield.
