Corn Disease Detection

Learn how to identify diseases affecting corn plants.

Common Corn Diseases

  • Cercospora Leaf Spot: Necrotic lesions that can be brown to gray.
  • Common Rust: Reddish-brown blisters on leaves.
  • Northern Leaf Blight: Dark, rectangular spots on leaves.

Methods for Disease Detection

  • Visual Inspection: Check leaves for visible symptoms.
  • Image Classification Using AI: Use deep learning models to classify images of corn leaves.

Benefits of Using Technology

  • Quick Diagnosis: Immediate feedback on plant health.
  • Accessibility: Mobile apps for farmers in remote areas.
  • Improved Yields: Early detection leads to timely interventions.
